About TerraVolt BioWorks

TerraVolt BioWorks is a student‑founded innovation group developing BioSolar Mycelium Grids — modular tiles that generate clean energy while restoring degraded land. Our mission is to merge biotechnology, renewable energy, and ecological design into one scalable solution.

Mission

Deliver scalable, low‑cost solutions that advance SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy) and SDG 15 (Life on Land), while creating local green jobs and resilient communities.
